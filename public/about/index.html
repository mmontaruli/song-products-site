About us

The world has many problems. Here are our simple solutions.

Essentially Smooth

Essentially Smooth is a skin care company, offering top quality sugar scrubs (skin exfoliants) for use in the shower or bath. Essentially Smooth's products are all-natural and are raved about by their customers as being essential to their skin care routine.

Entre-Propel.com

Entre-Propel.com is an entrepreneurial blog dedicated to delivering words of motivation to its readers. Entrepreneurship can be emotionally strenuous, energy consuming and monetarily unrewarding in the early stages of a business's life. Entre-Propel.com strives to combat this by helping entrepreneurs identify the perks and rewards of entrepreneurship, regardless of a business's size.

MTM Consulting

MTM Consulting consults new and small businesses on ways to streamline operations and reduce operating expenses. MTM Consulting has worked with businesses in industries ranging from Film Production, Accounting and Retail.

For more information on our businesses listed above, or our other ventures, please contact us.
